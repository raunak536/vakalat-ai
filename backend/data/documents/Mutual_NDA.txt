
MUTUAL NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT

This Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into on October 01, 2025 between:

Party A: [Company/Individual Name]
Party B: [Company/Individual Name]

1. PURPOSE
Both parties may share confidential information for the purpose of [business purpose].

2. CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION DEFINITION
Confidential information includes:
- Technical data and specifications
- Business plans and strategies
- Financial information and projections
- Customer and supplier information
- Intellectual property and trade secrets
- Any other information marked as confidential

3. MUTUAL OBLIGATIONS
Each party agrees to:
- Maintain strict confidentiality of the other party's information
- Not use confidential information for any purpose other than [specific purpose]
- Not disclose information to third parties without written consent
- Implement reasonable security measures to protect confidential information

4. RETURN OF INFORMATION
Upon termination of this agreement or upon request, each party shall:
- Return all confidential materials
- Delete electronic copies
- Provide written certification of compliance

5. INJUNCTIVE RELIEF
Both parties acknowledge that monetary damages may be inadequate and agree that injunctive relief may be appropriate.

6. TERM AND TERMINATION
This agreement shall remain in effect for [duration] years or until terminated by either party with 30 days written notice.

7. GENERAL PROVISIONS
- This agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the parties
- No modification shall be effective unless in writing
- This agreement shall be governed by [Jurisdiction] law
